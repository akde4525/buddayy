Happy Birthday, Rishika!

Wishing you a year ahead filled with endless opportunities, love, and all the happiness in the world. Cheers to you, Misti Bondhu!, and the beautiful soul you are! üåü

In the heart of the gentle dawn's first light,
There shines a soul, pure and bright,
Rishika, a name that echoes grace,
In every step, in every trace.

On this day, as candles glow,
I celebrate you, dear friend, I know,
Your presence, a gift, so rare,
A treasure beyond compare.

Happy birthday, Rishika, dear,
May joy surround you, year after year,
May love and dreams forever flow,
In the garden of your heart, may they grow.

With each sunrise and every night,
May your path be bathed in light,
For you deserve the sweetest things,
Happy birthday, with love that sings.
